Behind the Score

How Each Dish Gets Its Score

Every dish in our database gets an AI Score out of 100. The score is built from three things we know customers actually care about.

A place with slightly fewer stars but thousands of glowing reviews will still rank higher than a place with 5 stars and just 3 reviews. The score is designed to reflect real crowd opinion, not just a number.

Score = (0.40 × Star Rating) + (0.40 × Review Positivity) + (0.20 × Number of Reviews)

All three values are scaled to 0–100 before combining, so they're fairly compared.
